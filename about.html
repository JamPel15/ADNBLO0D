About ADN Blood Link

Connecting lives, saving lives. Our mission is to link blood donors, hospitals, and patients across Agusan del Norte for a healthier, safer community.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, ADN Blood Link began as a community-driven initiative in Agusan del Norte, Philippines, aiming to solve the challenge of timely, safe, and accessible blood donation. We work hand-in-hand with local hospitals, volunteers, and health officials to build a network that empowers donors, supports patients, and saves lives.

Mission

To connect, inspire, and mobilize blood donors, ensuring every patient in need receives lifesaving blood quickly and safely.

Vision

A province where no life is lost due to blood shortage and where communities unite to save each other.

Values

Compassion, Transparency, Community, Reliability, and Innovation guide every action we take.
