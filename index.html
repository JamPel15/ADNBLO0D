How Blood Donation Works

Simple steps to save lives through blood donation

1. Register

Create an account on ADN Blood Link or sign in if you already have one. Complete your profile with necessary health information.

2. Schedule

Choose a hospital near you and schedule a convenient time for your blood donation. You'll receive confirmation and reminders.

3. Donate

Visit the hospital at your scheduled time. The donation process takes about 10-15 minutes, and you'll be helping save lives.

How to Request Blood

Quick steps to request blood for patients in need

Regular Request

  • 1

    Create an account or sign in to your existing account

  • 2

    Fill out the blood request form with patient details and required documentation

  • 3

    Select the hospital where the patient is admitted

  • 4

    Submit the request and wait for approval (usually within 24 hours)

Emergency Request

  • 1

    Click the "Emergency Request" button on the homepage

  • 2

    Provide essential patient information and hospital details

  • 3

    Submit the emergency request (verification may be done by phone)

  • 4

    Receive immediate notification when donors respond
